## Frequently asked questions

**How do I cancel a subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account. Tiny Treasure Grid doesn't store any subscription information.

**How do I restore my purchases on a new device?**
Tap "Restore purchases" in the app's settings or paywall. Your App Store / Google Play account must be the same one you originally bought with.

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device. Tiny Treasure Grid doesn't have a server. Nothing you enter leaves the phone.
