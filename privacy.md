# Privacy Policy — Tiny Treasure Grid

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

Tiny Treasure Grid is built around a simple promise: **what happens on your phone stays on your phone.**

## Data we collect

**None.** Tiny Treasure Grid does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

- We have no server.
- We use no analytics SDKs.
- We use no advertising SDKs.
- We do not include any third-party tracking.

## What happens locally on your device

Tiny Treasure Grid stores app state (your settings, history, scores, in-app purchase records) in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your phone. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted with it.

If you tap a Share button in the app, it opens your device's system share sheet so you can send content somewhere yourself. Tiny Treasure Grid does not initiate any sharing without your explicit tap.

## In-app purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Tiny Treasure Grid receives only the purchase result; it does not see your payment details. Purchase IDs are stored locally in order to validate restores and prevent double-grants.

## Children

Tiny Treasure Grid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update it here. Because we don't collect data, there's not much that can meaningfully change — but if Apple's policies require new disclosures, we'll add them.
